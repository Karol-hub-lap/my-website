About AfriLens Review

Our Mission

AfriLens Review was founded in 2025 with the goal of providing critical perspectives on African media through the lens of our rich cultural heritage, legal traditions, and contemporary development challenges. We believe that thoughtful media criticism can contribute to a more nuanced understanding of Africa's place in global cultural production.

Our Vision

We envision a world where African narratives are analyzed with the same depth and respect as Western media, where our cultural contexts inform critical frameworks, and where African audiences see themselves authentically represented in media criticism.

About the Founder

Antony Karol is a Kenyan cultural critic and legal scholar with a background in African literature and media studies. He is a Law student at Kabarak University.
